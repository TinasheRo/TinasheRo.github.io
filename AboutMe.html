Meet Roseanne

A curious product designer,
built to solve real problems.

Every product I design starts with understanding the people I'm designing for. This photo captures that philosophy. The tools aren't the main course, they're simply the ingredients I use to create meaningful experiences.

I'm a Product Designer who enjoys transforming complex problems into intuitive digital experiences. My work combines user research, interaction design and front-end development to bridge the gap between understanding users and building solutions that make a real difference.

Beyond designing interfaces, I love bringing ideas to life through storytelling and communication. I'm currently helping the LDN Research Trust grow its presence on LinkedIn and TikTok, using content to raise awareness, connect with new audiences and support fundraising efforts. Whether I'm designing an interface or creating a campaign, my goal is always the same: understand people first and create experiences that leave a lasting impact.
